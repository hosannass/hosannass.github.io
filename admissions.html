Admission Guidelines

S1 Admission Requirements

  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) Certificate
  • Birth Certificate or Statutory Declaration of Age
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical Certificate from a recognized health facility
  • Completed application form
  • Payment of application fee (UGX 50,000)

S5 Admission Requirements

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with minimum 5 passes
  • Academic transcripts from previous school
  • Letter of recommendation from previous school
  • Birth Certificate
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical Certificate
  • Completed application form
  • Payment of application fee (UGX 75,000)

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the application form with all required documents

2

Document Review

Our admissions team reviews your application and supporting documents

3

Interview/Assessment

Attend an interview or assessment session (if required)

4

Admission Decision

Receive notification of admission decision within 2 weeks

5

Enrollment

Complete enrollment by paying fees and attending orientation
